Our Story

Born from Real Immigration Challenges

Quimigrate.AI emerged from years of watching brilliant legal professionals drown in paperwork — and immigrants struggle to navigate a system not built for them.

Our Evolution

From Quimigrate to Quimigrate.AI

Quimigrate began as a flat-fee immigration service helping thousands of clients navigate U.S. immigration law. Along the way, we noticed a pattern: the same questions, the same forms, the same bottlenecks — repeated across every case.

We realized that much of the intake and form-preparation work could be intelligently automated without sacrificing accuracy or compliance. So we built an AI layer on top of our proven legal workflows — and Quimigrate.AI was born.

Today, our platform serves both individual applicants and law firms across all 50 states, processing thousands of immigration forms each month with industry-leading accuracy.
